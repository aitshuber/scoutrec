Our History

In 2018, our partners came together with the idea to build a new commercial real estate model. We wanted to service Lafayette and shape lasting relationships with client-partners looking to succeed, while also enhancing the quality of life in their local community. Jeff Landry and Hammy Davis founded Scout Real Estate Co., a full-service commercial real estate, brokerage, management and consulting firm based on honesty, integrity and a rock-solid work ethic. In 2021, our leadership and management team was rounded out with the addition of Rex Moroux, one of Acadiana’s foremost experts on commercial real estate, with an emphasis on downtown properties.

Scout is less worried about the number of signs we put in the ground and more committed to working on projects that have a significant impact for our clients and our community. We are experienced and we have a deep network that comes from years of hard work in the commercial real estate markets across South Louisiana. We provide our clients access to the most current technology and information all combined with old-school techniques such as actually answering the phone, meeting with people in person and dealing with challenges face-to-face. We know, it’s a radical approach in today’s market, but one that you will immediately appreciate.
